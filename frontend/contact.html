Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with StudySync?

Simply click on "Get Started" and choose whether you want to join as a student or mentor. Complete your profile and start connecting!

Is StudySync free to use?

Yes, our basic features are completely free. We also offer premium features for enhanced learning experiences.

How do I find the right mentor or study group?

Use our search and filter options to find mentors or study groups based on subject, availability, experience level, and location preferences.

What if I need technical support?

You can reach out to us through the contact form above, email us directly, or connect with our team members via their GitHub profiles.
